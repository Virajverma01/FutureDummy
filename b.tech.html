B.Tech CSE

About the Program

The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (B.Tech in CSE) program is a rigorous four year undergraduate journey that blends strong theoretical foundations with advanced technological applications. The program explores the complete spectrum of computing, from hardware architecture and programming logic to emerging domains such as artificial intelligence, cyber-security, database systems, and modern software development.

Eligibility & Fee Details

Candidate must have passed Intermediate (10+2) Examination with PCM, PCMB, or Maths & Physics as compulsory subjects with at least 45% marks (40% for SC/ST). Lateral Entry requires a Diploma in engineering or B.Sc. with Mathematics.

Admission Procedure

At Future University, admission to the B.Tech Programme is conducted through a structured, transparent, and merit-driven process designed to identify talented and motivated engineering aspirants.

We follow a competitive yet fair selection system that ensures equal opportunity for all eligible candidates.

Admissions are offered through one or more of the following routes:

1. Qualifying Examination Merit

  • Admission based on performance in 10+2 (PCM/Relevant Subjects)
  • Merit list prepared as per university eligibility norms
  • Applicable for selected seats

2. State & National Level Entrance Examinations

Candidates may secure admission through recognized entrance examinations conducted at:

  • State Level Entrance Exams (as notified by respective state authorities)
  • National Level Entrance Exams such as Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main

3. FUCET – Future University Common Entrance Test

Future University conducts its own entrance examination:

  • FUCET (Future University Common Entrance Test)
  • Candidates are shortlisted based on performance
  • Final selection may include counseling and document verification

Vision of the Department

To nurture competent computer science engineers who are innovative, ethically responsible, and capable of addressing global technological challenges through cutting-edge education and research. The department strives to foster critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning, empowering students to develop sustainable, secure, and impactful technological solutions for the betterment of society and the advancement of the global digital ecosystem.

About Us

Future University, Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, is legally established and recognized under the Uttar Pradesh Private Universities (Third Amendment) Act, 2024 (U.P. Act No. 12 of 2024) and has been granted approval under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956. This recognition enables the university to function as a degree-granting institution, ensuring compliance with national higher education standards set by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and other statutory bodies.
